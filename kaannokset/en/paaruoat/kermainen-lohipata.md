# Creamy salmon casserole

## Description

This is an English translation draft for the pilot recipe used to test the multilingual content model in real files.

## Ingredients

- 600 g salmon fillet
- 1 small onion
- 2 garlic cloves
- 200 g cream cheese
- 2 dl heavy cream
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tsp salt
- 0.5 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp dried dill
- 150 g spinach

## Preparation

1. Preheat the oven to 200 C.
2. Finely chop the onion and garlic. Saute them in butter.
3. Add cream cheese, cream, lemon juice, mustard and seasonings. Stir into a smooth sauce.
4. Fold the spinach briefly into the sauce.
5. Place the salmon in an oven dish and pour the sauce over it.
6. Bake for about 25 minutes until the salmon is just cooked.
7. Let it rest briefly before serving.

## Nutrition estimate per serving

- net carbs: about 6 g
- total carbs: about 8 g
- protein: about 29 g
- fat: about 35 g
