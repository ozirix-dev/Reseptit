# Mushroom Cake Base

This mushroom cake base is soft and airy, and it works especially well as a base for filled cakes.

## Description

In this Finnish sponge-style cake, boiling water is added to the egg-sugar foam. That makes the foam lighter and gives the cake base an airy, sponge-like structure. The result is very soft, but slightly more fragile than a regular sponge cake base.

## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 2 dl sugar
- 1 dl water
- 3 dl all-purpose flour
- 1 1/2 tsp baking powder

## Preparation

1. Preheat the oven to 175 C. Grease and breadcrumb a 24 cm round cake pan.
2. Whip the room-temperature eggs and sugar into a pale, thick foam.
3. Bring the water to a boil. Slowly add the boiling water to the foam while whisking vigorously.
4. Combine the flour and baking powder. Sift the dry ingredients into the foam and gently fold until smooth. Do not whisk anymore at this stage.
5. Pour the batter into the pan and bake on the lower or middle rack for about 35 minutes. Check doneness with a skewer.

## Notes

- Adding boiling water makes the base noticeably lighter.
- Use a large bowl, because the foam expands quite a lot when the water is added.
